Neighborhood-map - ReactJS

Designed and developed an interactive full-page map that loads with popular locations near North London. Integrated Foursquare API to show pictures and reviews of the best locations loaded in the map (retrieved by AJAX requests). Identified the best accessibility practices and implemented a mobile-first approach to improve usability.
